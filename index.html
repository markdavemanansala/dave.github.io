I am Dave

I'm a passionate blockchain advocate and crypto enthusiast. I'm also a dedicated programmer and have worked with several companies and created numerous projects. Started my programming career when I was in the university as a freelance web and mobile developer and as a project manager. I graduated with a degree in Computer Science from Notre Dame of Dadiangas University. Prior to taking this role, I worked as Operations Manager in a crypto company called SnipersTube based in Los Angeles, California. I was often invited to talk in different Blockchain Conferences in my country and very passionate in spreading the word about cryptocurrency and blockchain technology.
I firmly believes that blockchain technology is one of today’s biggest ground-breaking technologies which has the potential to disrupt every industry, from financial institutions to educational, healthcare, transportation, manufacturing, entertainment and a lot more. With blockchain, I hopes to see innovations that aren’t even thought of yet to emerge and address our varied needs and I'm very excited to see more people getting to know and adopt this revolutionary technology.
