I am Dave

I'm a passionate blockchain advocate and crypto enthusiast. I have worked with several companies and created numerous projects. I started my programming career when I was in the university as a freelance web and mobile developer and as a project manager. I graduated with a degree in Computer Science from Notre Dame of Dadiangas University. Prior to taking this field, I worked as Operations Manager in a crypto company called SnipersTube based in Los Angeles, California. Now, I am running a Coinserves which is now called Blocksaves, I am also advising on several crypto companies and was often invited to talk in different Blockchain Conferences and very passionate in spreading the word about cryptocurrency and blockchain technology. Overall, I have 7 years experience in the Software Development, Business and Marketing field.

I firmly believe that blockchain technology is one of today’s biggest ground-breaking technologies that have the potential to disrupt every industry, from financial institutions to educational, healthcare, transportation, manufacturing, entertainment and a lot more.
